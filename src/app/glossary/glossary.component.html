Models Definition The way to charge per each Action
CPC Cost Per Click

a pricing model where companies are charged for every click people make on a displayed/test ad which leads people to your company’s website. All (Automotive, Banks, Insurance, Real Estate, Health, Education, Research, Travel, E-Commerce, VAS)
CPL Cost Per Lead

a pricing model where companies are charged only when leads are generated. All except VAS & Mobile Apps
CPVL ............ Real Estate only
CPA Cost Per Action

a pricing model where companies are charged by advertising platforms only when leads, sales or conversions are generated, VAS only
CPI Cost Per Install

a pricing model where companies are charged by advertising platforms only when install are generated for their app (IOS / Android) or download through desktop
